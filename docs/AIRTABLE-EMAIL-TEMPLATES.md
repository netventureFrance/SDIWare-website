## How to Use These Templates in Airtable

### **Step 1: Go to Your Automation**

1. Open Airtable â†’ Your base
2. Click **Automations** (top toolbar)
3. Find your download email automation
4. Click to edit it

### **Step 2: Update Email Action**

1. Find the **Send email** action
2. Click to edit
3. **From**: `info@sdiware.video` or your verified email
4. **To**: `{Email}` (field from record)
5. **Subject**: Copy subject line from template above
6. **Message**: Copy email body from template above

### **Step 3: Test**

1. Click **Test** button in automation
2. Select a test record
3. Check if email looks good
4. Turn automation **ON**
